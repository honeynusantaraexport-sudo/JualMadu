Quality Control & Product Safety

We apply strict quality control procedures to ensure that our honey meets export-quality standards and buyer requirements.

Raw Material Selection

  • Sourced from trusted Indonesian beekeepers
  • No additives or artificial processing
  • Natural harvesting environment

Processing & Handling

  • Hygienic handling procedures
  • Food-grade containers
  • Controlled storage conditions

Laboratory Testing

Laboratory analysis such as moisture content, purity, and safety testing can be arranged upon buyer request.

Certificates (Available Upon Request)

  • Certificate of Analysis (COA)
  • Health Certificate
  • Halal Certificate (if required)
  • MSDS (for logistics purposes)

Specific certificates are provided based on buyer requirements and destination country regulations.
