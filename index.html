About Nusantara Honey Export

Nusantara Honey Export is an Indonesian honey supplier committed to delivering high-quality natural honey from the rich biodiversity of Nusantara to the global market. Our honey is harvested from bees that thrive in their natural environment, without additives or artificial processing, preserving its authentic taste, aroma, and natural nutrients. We apply strict raw material selection and hygienic handling processes to ensure purity, safety, and consistent quality in every shipment

With a reliable supply chain and export-ready standards, Nusantara Honey Export is ready to become a trusted partner for international buyers.
